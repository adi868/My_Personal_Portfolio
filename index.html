About Me


Hi! My name is Adina Cianciotto. I am a Northwestern graduate, Chicago-native, cat-mom, and Full-Stack Web Developer who fell in love with the art and science of coding. To me, web development is a passion and joy. It's a platform to express creatively, never stop learning, and work together to bring bright ideas to life. Now I am driven by the passion to help others build applications with meaningful, clean design, and help bring their ideas to life. When I'm not studying the latest tech or testing code, you can find me with my cats, reading a good book, playing tennis, or listening to some of my favorite podcasts. Welcome to my site!
